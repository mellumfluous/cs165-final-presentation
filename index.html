How do you grade your life?

Purpose of the project:

The OECD (Organization for Economic Cooperation and Development) is a group of democratic countries that try to promote economic growth, prosperity, and sustainable development. The hope for this project is to provide a visualization for the countries participating and where they stand compared to each other in regards to the many criterion involved in calculating the Better Life Index.
